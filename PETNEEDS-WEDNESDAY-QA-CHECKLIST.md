# PetNeeds.ai Wednesday Launch QA Checklist

Launch date: Wednesday, June 3, 2026

## Goal

Confirm PetNeeds.ai is safe enough for first public promotion.

## Pages to check first

Open each page on desktop and mobile:

- `https://petneeds.ai/`
- `https://petneeds.ai/starter-guides.html`
- `https://petneeds.ai/pet-family-notes-guide.html`
- `https://petneeds.ai/emergency.html`
- `https://petneeds.ai/upload-photo.html`
- `https://petneeds.ai/advertise.html`
- `https://petneeds.ai/local-pet-resources.html`
- `https://petneeds.ai/privacy.html`
- `https://petneeds.ai/terms.html`
- `https://petneeds.ai/affiliate-disclosure.html`

## Homepage check

- Main headline is clear.
- Starter guide button works.
- Family notes button works.
- Featured guide links work.
- No fake live email signup promise appears.
- Footer links work.
- Safety language appears in footer.

## Photo page check

- Page says photos are reviewed before public display.
- Page does not promise instant posting.
- Page does not include an uncontrolled upload tool.
- Rules are family-friendly and clear.

## Advertise page check

- Page explains partner listings and commission options.
- Paid relationships are described as clearly disclosed.
- No live payment button exists.
- No tracking script is added.
- Email link works.

## Safety check

- Emergency page is easy to find.
- PetNeeds.ai does not claim to diagnose or treat pets.
- Content points urgent concerns to a veterinarian or emergency clinic.

## Mobile check

- Navigation wraps or fits.
- Cards are readable.
- Buttons are easy to tap.
- Footer is readable.
- No section is wider than the screen.

## Launch approval

Launch is ready when:

- main links work
- safety page is visible
- photo page is review-only
- advertiser page is inquiry-only
- no fake live tools are presented as active
