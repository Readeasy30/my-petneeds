# PetNeeds.ai Advertiser Outreach

## Goal

Find early advertisers and direct-commission partners that fit PetNeeds.ai's family-friendly, pet-safe purpose.

## Best first targets

Start with smaller partners because they can make decisions faster.

Best first outreach groups:

- local pet stores
- groomers
- dog trainers
- boarding services
- pet sitters
- aquarium stores
- bird supply stores
- reptile supply stores
- pet photographers
- rescue-friendly local businesses

## Offer 1 — Partner listing

A clearly labeled partner listing on PetNeeds.ai.

Good for local businesses that want visibility.

## Offer 2 — Promo-code commission

The partner gives PetNeeds.ai a unique promo code.

When a customer uses that code, the partner pays PetNeeds.ai a commission.

This avoids adding tracking scripts during the first launch stage.

## Offer 3 — Sponsored guide mention

A partner sponsors a helpful guide page.

Example: a senior pet comfort guide could include a clearly labeled sponsor note from a pet bed company or local pet store.

## Outreach email

Subject: PetNeeds.ai partner opportunity

Hi,

I’m building PetNeeds.ai, a family-friendly pet care website with simple guides for dogs, cats, fish, birds, reptiles, small pets, senior pets, and rescue pets.

We are preparing a small partner program for pet-safe products and local pet services.

The first version is simple: a clearly labeled partner listing, sponsored guide mention, or promo-code commission if that fits your business.

PetNeeds.ai focuses on helpful, beginner-friendly pet information and clear safety language. We do not publish fake reviews or unsupported pet health claims.

Would you be interested in being considered as an early PetNeeds.ai partner?

Thank you,
Gerry
PetNeeds.ai

## Review before contacting

Before outreach, check:

- product or service looks pet-safe
- no extreme or unsupported health claims
- website looks active
- contact method exists
- business fits family pet owners
- disclosure would be easy to explain
