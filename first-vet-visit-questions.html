First Vet Visit Questions

A short question list helps new pet owners make the first vet visit more useful and less stressful.

Starter ChecklistSafety Guide

Bring notes

Write down food, behavior, bathroom changes, records, and questions before the visit.

Pet safety note

This page gives general pet-care information. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace licensed veterinary care.

Food questions

  • What food is right for this pet?
  • How much should I feed?
  • How often should meals happen?
  • How should I change food safely?

Health routine questions

  • What vaccines or preventive care should I ask about?
  • What records should I keep?
  • What changes should I watch for at home?
  • When should the next visit happen?

Behavior and home questions

  • What daily routine is best?
  • What bathroom habits should I track?
  • What home setup changes would help?
  • When should I ask for behavior help?

Bring to the visit

  • adoption, rescue, shelter, or breeder papers
  • current food name
  • photos of labels or notes if useful
  • your written question list
