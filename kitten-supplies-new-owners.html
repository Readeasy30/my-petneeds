Kitten starter supplies

Kitten Supplies for New Owners

A new kitten needs a calm starter room, clean litter setup, safe food, a carrier, and simple first-week routines.

New Kitten Checklist Litter Box Basics

Start simple

Prepare food, bowls, litter box, carrier, scratching post, safe toys, pet-safe cleaner, and a quiet starter room.

Keep it calm

Set up one quiet starter space before bringing the kitten home. Simple rooms are easier for kittens and families.

Basics first

Simple kitten supply list

Food and water

Use kitten food unless a qualified pet-care professional recommends something different. Keep clean water available.

Litter setup

Prepare a litter box, litter, scoop, waste bags, and a quiet location.

Carrier

Have a carrier ready for pickup day, appointments, and travel.

Scratch and play

Add a scratching post or pad and safe toys. Avoid loose string when no adult is watching.

Starter room

Give the kitten one quiet space.

Food, water, litter box, bed, hiding spot, toys, and scratching surface help the kitten adjust.

Cleaning

Keep supplies easy to reach.

Pet-safe cleaner, a litter scoop, waste bags, and washable bedding make daily care easier.
