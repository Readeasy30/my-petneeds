Simple care help for families with rabbits, guinea pigs, hamsters, gerbils, mice, rats, and other small pets.

Best small pet care rule

Small pets need calm handling, clean homes, correct food, safe bedding, fresh water, and a routine the whole family understands.

Small pet safety reminder

For not eating, trouble breathing, injury, weakness, diarrhea, severe itching, bloating, bleeding, or fast behavior changes, contact an exotic-pet veterinarian or emergency clinic.

Rabbit and Guinea Pig Basics

These pets need room, gentle handling, steady cleaning, fresh water, and species-specific food. Do not treat all small pets the same.

Track care notes

Hamster Home Basics

Plan bedding depth, hiding places, safe wheel size, chewing needs, quiet sleep time, and careful handling close to the floor.

Children and Small Pets

Teach slow hands, calm voices, supervised holding, and no squeezing. Small pets can be hurt by rough handling.
