Care basics

Better Bird Setup

Safe Cage

Use a cage with room to move, safe spacing, and no sharp edges.

Clean Water

Change water daily and clean bowls often.

Daily Attention

Talk gently and build trust slowly.

Fresh Air

Keep birds away from smoke, strong fumes, and unsafe household air.
