Privacy Policy

This privacy page explains how PetNeeds.ai should treat visitor information as the site grows.

Plain-language privacy promise

PetNeeds.ai should collect only what is needed, explain why it is needed, and keep visitor trust ahead of growth features.

Information Visitors Provide

Visitors may choose to share information through email, contact messages, future question tools, or future photo review features.

No Sale of Personal Information

PetNeeds.ai does not sell personal information. Future tools should be added only when privacy and disclosure rules are clear.

Future Analytics and Ads

If analytics, partner links, or advertising are added later, this page should be updated before those tools go live.

Children and Families

PetNeeds.ai is family-friendly, but adults should supervise children when sharing photos, stories, or contact information.

Photo Review Features

Future pet photo features should use a review-first process. Photos should not appear publicly without review.
