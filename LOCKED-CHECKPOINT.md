## Locked photo submission status

The PetNeeds.ai photo upload flow is locked as an email-based submission process.

Live public upload routes:

- `https://petneeds.ai/upload-photo`
- `https://petneeds.ai/upload-photo.html`

Current public page status text:

- `Status: email pet photo submission is open.`

Current official submission method:

- Visitors click `Email Pet Photo`.
- Email goes to `petneedsai@gmail.com`.
- The email template asks for first name, last initial, pet name, pet type, caption, permission, consent, and safety confirmation.
- Visitors attach one clear pet photo.
- Photos are reviewed before anything appears publicly.

## Locked photo safety rules

- Photos do not publish automatically.
- Human review is required before public display.
- Photos should be pet-focused, family-friendly, and safe.
- No private information should be visible.
- No unsafe animal handling should be shown.
- People should not be shown unless clear permission is documented.
- Submission does not guarantee publication.
