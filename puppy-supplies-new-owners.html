Puppy Supplies for New Owners

Start with safe basics before buying extras. A puppy needs food, water, ID, leash, sleep space, cleanup supplies, and simple routines.

New Puppy ChecklistAll Pet Supplies

Buying rule

Choose supplies that fit your puppy’s size, age, and daily routine.

Pet safety note

This page gives general pet-care information. It does not replace licensed veterinary care.

Basics first

Simple puppy supply list

Food and bowls

Use puppy food that fits age and size. Keep fresh water available.

Leash and ID

Have a collar or harness, leash, and ID tag ready before pickup day.

Sleep space

Prepare a crate, pen, or quiet safe area with washable bedding.

Cleanup

Keep pet-safe cleaner, poop bags, towels, and simple cleanup tools nearby.

Chew and play

Pick safe toys.

Use safe chew toys and avoid toys that are too small, sharp, broken, or easy to swallow.

Vet questions

Write questions before the visit.

Ask about food amount, vaccines, growth, parasite prevention, and when to call for help.
