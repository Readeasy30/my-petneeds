Bird Cage Setup Basics

A simple guide for setting up a calmer bird space at home.

Cage Location

  • Choose a calm room
  • Avoid smoke and strong smells
  • Keep away from drafts
  • Avoid direct hot sunlight
  • Keep the cage easy to check

Daily Basics

  • Fresh water
  • Clean food dish
  • Safe toys
  • Clean cage bottom
  • Gentle daily attention

Family Job

Pick one person to check water and one person to check the cage area each day.
