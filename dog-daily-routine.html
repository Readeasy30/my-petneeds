Dog Daily Routine Guide

A steady routine helps many dogs feel calmer and helps families notice changes sooner.

Morning Routine

  • Fresh water
  • Breakfast
  • Bathroom break or walk
  • Short calm play
  • Rest time

Evening Routine

  • Dinner
  • Walk or gentle activity
  • Quiet time
  • Bedtime bathroom break
  • Same sleeping spot when possible

Family Notes

Write down meals, water, bathroom habits, sleep, energy, and unusual changes. Simple notes help the family stay consistent.
