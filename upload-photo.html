Pet Photo Ideas and Review Rules

PetNeeds.ai may add reviewed pet photo features later. Photos will not be posted automatically. Every public photo must be reviewed first for safety, kindness, and family-friendly content.

Community Rules

Future stories should be kind, family-friendly, and focused on the pet. Photos should not show private information, unsafe handling, or offensive content.

Before a photo can be public

  • The pet should be the main subject.
  • The photo should be family-friendly.
  • No private information should be visible.
  • No unsafe animal handling should be shown.
  • People should not be shown unless clear permission is documented.
  • The caption should be kind and non-offensive.

Why review matters

PetNeeds.ai is built for families and everyday pet owners. A review step protects visitors, pet owners, children, and the pets being featured.

Photo tools will stay planning-only until the review process is ready.

Future community tools and review steps will be planned before anything public is added. PetNeeds.ai will not use instant public posting for pet photos.
