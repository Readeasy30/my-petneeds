AI Photo Check

AI can help screen pet photos before human review. It can look for problems like adult content, violence, unsafe handling, visible private information, blurry images, screenshots, memes, non-pet images, or photos that do not match the selected pet category.

AI is only a helper. A person should still approve the final photo before it appears on PetNeeds.ai.

Community Rules

Photos and captions should be kind, family-friendly, and focused on the pet. Photos should not show private information, unsafe handling, offensive content, or anything that could put a child, owner, or pet at risk.

Before a Photo Can Be Public

  • The pet should be the main subject.
  • The photo should be family-friendly.
  • No private information should be visible.
  • No unsafe animal handling should be shown.
  • People should not be shown unless clear permission is documented.
  • The caption should be kind and non-offensive.
  • AI can flag problems, but a human review must happen before public display.

Review Decision

  • Approve: safe, clear, pet-focused, and permission is complete.
  • Needs review: unclear pet type, people shown, poor image quality, or caption needs editing.
  • Reject: unsafe, offensive, adult, violent, private, spam, or not pet-related.
Better final version: connect a Google Form with one image upload, responses saved to Google Sheets, and manual approval before photos are published.
