Community Rules

Photos and captions should be kind, family-friendly, and focused on the pet. Photos should not show private information, unsafe handling, offensive content, or anything that could put a child, owner, or pet at risk.

Before a photo can be public

  • The pet should be the main subject.
  • The photo should be family-friendly.
  • No private information should be visible.
  • No unsafe animal handling should be shown.
  • People should not be shown unless clear permission is documented.
  • The caption should be kind and non-offensive.
  • A human review must happen before public display.

Why review matters

PetNeeds.ai is built for families and everyday pet owners. A review step protects visitors, pet owners, children, and the pets being featured.

Instant public posting is not allowed. Review first, publish later.
