New Pet First Week Routine

A simple first-week routine for families bringing home a new pet.

Day One Setup

  • Choose one quiet rest space
  • Show food and water areas
  • Keep the first day calm
  • Limit visitors
  • Write down first-day notes

Daily Rhythm

  • Morning food and water check
  • Short activity or gentle play
  • Rest time
  • Evening food and water check
  • Same bedtime pattern when possible

Family Jobs

Give each person one simple job. One person checks water, one checks food, one handles cleanup, and one writes daily notes.
