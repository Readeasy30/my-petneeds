# PetNeeds.ai Photo Submission Form Fields

## Purpose

Create a controlled pet photo submission process where nothing becomes public until reviewed.

Best first tool: Google Form connected to a spreadsheet.

## Form title

PetNeeds.ai Reviewed Pet Photo Submission

## Form description

Share a pet photo for possible future PetNeeds.ai community features. Photos are reviewed before anything is published. Submission does not guarantee publication.

## Required fields

### Owner information

- First name
- Last initial only
- Email address

### Pet information

- Pet name
- Pet type
- Pet age if known
- Pet category

Pet category options:

- Dog
- Cat
- Fish
- Bird
- Reptile
- Small pet
- Senior pet
- Rescue pet
- Other

### Photo upload

- Upload one image
- Image files only
- Recommended max size: 10 MB

### Caption

Prompt:

Tell us one kind, family-friendly sentence about your pet.

### Permission checkboxes

Require these checkboxes:

- I own this photo or have permission to share it.
- I give PetNeeds.ai permission to review this photo for possible public display.
- I understand submission does not guarantee publication.
- I understand PetNeeds.ai may edit the caption for clarity, kindness, or safety.
- I confirm the photo is family-friendly.
- I confirm the photo does not show private information.
- I confirm the photo does not show unsafe animal handling.
- I confirm no people are clearly shown unless I have permission for them to appear.

## Optional fields

- City/state, optional
- Rescue story, optional
- Product shown in photo, optional
- Permission to contact me for follow-up, optional

## Review spreadsheet columns

- Date received
- First name
- Last initial
- Email
- Pet name
- Pet type
- Caption
- File link
- Permission confirmed
- Review status
- Reviewer notes
- Approved caption
- Publish category
- Publish date
- Removal request

## Review status values

- New
- Needs review
- Approved
- Needs edit
- Rejected
- Published
- Removed

## Public display rule

Use approved public display names only.

Example:

Buddy the rescue dog

Do not publish submitter full names, email addresses, home addresses, or private details.
