Senior pet checklist

Senior Pet Comfort Checklist

A simple home checklist for families caring for an older pet.

Senior pet safety reminder

For trouble breathing, collapse, severe pain, injury, seizures, poisoning risk, not eating, repeated vomiting, sudden weakness, or fast behavior changes, contact a veterinarian or emergency clinic.

Open the safety guide →

Home Setup

  • Soft bed
  • Easy water access
  • Clear walking path
  • Non-slip areas when possible
  • Quiet rest area
  • Gentle daily routine

Daily Notes

  • Meals
  • Water
  • Sleep
  • Bathroom habits
  • Activity
  • Mood or behavior changes

Comfort Checks

  • Walking or stiffness changes
  • Less jumping or climbing
  • New hiding or rest spots
  • Appetite changes
  • Weight or body changes
  • New sounds or signs of pain
