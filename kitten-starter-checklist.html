Kitten Starter Checklist

A calm first-week checklist for families bringing home a kitten. Start with one safe room, food, water, litter, rest, and gentle play.

Open Kitten Supplies Litter Box Basics

First-week rule

Keep the first week quiet and predictable. Kittens adjust better when the home feels calm and easy to understand.

Start with one room

A small starter room can help a kitten find food, water, litter, rest, and safe hiding places without feeling overwhelmed.

Setup

  • Food and water bowls
  • Litter box and litter
  • Quiet starter room
  • Carrier
  • Simple toys
  • Scratching post or pad

Daily Routine

  • Meals
  • Fresh water
  • Litter box cleaning
  • Gentle play time
  • Rest time
  • Short family check-ins

Family Jobs

Give each person one simple job. Kittens do best when the home routine is calm and steady.

  • One person checks food
  • One person checks water
  • One adult checks litter
  • Children use calm hands
