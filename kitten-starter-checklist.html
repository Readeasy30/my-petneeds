Kitten Starter Checklist

A calm first-week checklist for families bringing home a kitten.

Kitten safety reminder

For trouble breathing, injury, poisoning risk, repeated vomiting, no urination, not eating, collapse, or sudden behavior changes, contact a veterinarian or emergency clinic.

Open the safety guide →

Setup

  • Food and water bowls
  • Litter box and litter
  • Quiet starter room
  • Carrier
  • Simple toys
  • Scratching post

Daily Routine

  • Meals
  • Fresh water
  • Litter box cleaning
  • Gentle play time
  • Rest time
  • Short family check-ins

Family Jobs

Give each person one simple job. Kittens do best when the home routine is calm and steady.

  • One person checks food
  • One person checks water
  • One adult checks litter
  • Children use calm hands
