Dog Comfort Checklist

Use this simple checklist when a dog seems unsettled, tired, worried, uncomfortable, or affected by a change in routine.

Dog safety reminder

For sudden pain, breathing trouble, injury, poisoning risk, repeated vomiting, collapse, seizures, or fast behavior changes, contact a veterinarian or emergency clinic.

Open the safety guide →

Check the Setting

  • Room temperature
  • Wet fur
  • Loud noises
  • New people or pets
  • Recent travel
  • Less rest than usual

Check Daily Basics

  • Fresh water
  • Normal meals
  • Bathroom routine
  • Walk or potty rhythm
  • Safe rest area
  • Favorite comfort item

Calm First Steps

  • Offer a quiet place
  • Keep voices calm
  • Use a steady routine
  • Write down what changed
  • Avoid crowding the dog
  • Ask for help if signs get worse
