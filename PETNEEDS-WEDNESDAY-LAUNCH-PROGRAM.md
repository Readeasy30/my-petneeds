# PetNeeds.ai Wednesday Launch Program

Launch date: Wednesday, June 3, 2026

## Launch goal

Start public promotion safely with helpful pet-care content, family-friendly trust language, and no risky automation.

## Main launch message

PetNeeds.ai helps families care for pets with simple starter guides, safety reminders, pet category pages, and practical notes for everyday pet routines.

## Main launch destinations

Use these pages first:

1. `https://petneeds.ai/`
2. `https://petneeds.ai/starter-guides.html`
3. `https://petneeds.ai/pet-family-notes-guide.html`
4. `https://petneeds.ai/emergency.html`
5. `https://petneeds.ai/upload-photo.html`
6. `https://petneeds.ai/local-pet-resources.html`

## Launch priorities

### Priority 1 — Trust

Lead with safety and usefulness.

Use this sentence often:

PetNeeds.ai shares general pet information for learning and planning. It does not replace licensed veterinary care.

### Priority 2 — Starter guides

Promote starter guides first because they are practical and low-risk.

Best launch angle:

New pet at home? Start with a simple checklist before the first week gets chaotic.

### Priority 3 — Family notes

Promote the family notes guide as the first practical downloadable/printable-style idea.

Best launch angle:

Keep your pet routine, symptoms, questions, and care notes in one place.

### Priority 4 — Photo community planning

Do not launch public auto-posting of pet photos yet.

Promote the photo page as a future community feature and safety-first review process.

## Wednesday launch checklist

### Before posting

- Open the homepage on desktop.
- Open the homepage on mobile.
- Check the starter guides page.
- Check the pet family notes guide.
- Check the emergency/safety page.
- Check the photo ideas page.
- Check footer links.
- Confirm no fake upload tool is visible as live.
- Confirm no fake email signup promises are live.

### First posts

Post these in this order:

1. Facebook personal/page post
2. Pinterest pin for starter guides
3. Instagram/Facebook reel or image post
4. LinkedIn founder-style post
5. Reddit-safe feedback post only where allowed
6. Local pet group post only if group rules allow it

## Launch content rules

Do:

- Be helpful.
- Use plain language.
- Invite feedback.
- Make safety language clear.
- Say the photo feature is planned/reviewed, not automatic.

Do not:

- Make diagnosis claims.
- Promise veterinary answers.
- Say uploads publish instantly.
- Add affiliate claims before partner review.
- Add local sponsor claims before local sponsor approval.

## First week success measure

Track these manually:

- visits by page from Cloudflare analytics
- feedback comments
- pages people ask for next
- photo feature interest
- local sponsor interest
- broken links or confusing pages
