Senior Pet Daily Comfort

A simple guide for helping older pets keep a steady, comfortable daily routine.

Comfort Setup

  • Soft bed
  • Easy water access
  • Clear walking path
  • Quiet rest area
  • Non-slip floor support if needed

Daily Notes

  • Meals
  • Water
  • Sleep
  • Activity
  • Mood

Family Routine

Keep meals, rest, and gentle activity steady. Small notes can help the family notice patterns sooner.
