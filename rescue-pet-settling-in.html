Rescue Pet Settling In Guide

A calm guide for the first days at home with a rescue pet.

Keep It Simple

  • Use one quiet rest space
  • Keep visitors low at first
  • Use slow voices and simple routines
  • Let the pet explore at their pace
  • Write down daily notes

Family Routine

  • One person handles meals
  • One person checks water
  • One person handles cleanup
  • Children use calm voices
  • Everyone gives the pet space

What to Notice

Notice eating, drinking, sleep, bathroom habits, activity, and what helps the pet relax.
