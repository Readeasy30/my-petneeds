# PetNeeds.ai First 10 Advertiser Targets

## Purpose

Build the first small advertiser outreach list for PetNeeds.ai without adding live ads, payment tools, or tracking scripts.

## Best first target types

Start with businesses that are easy to explain and useful to pet families.

## First 10 target categories

1. Local independent pet store
2. Local dog groomer
3. Local dog trainer
4. Local boarding or day care service
5. Local pet sitter or dog walker
6. Aquarium supply store
7. Reptile supply store
8. Bird supply store
9. Pet photographer
10. Pet-safe product brand with a direct contact page

## What to look for

Before contacting, check:

- active website
- clear contact method
- family-friendly business
- pet-safe products or services
- no unsupported health claims
- no harsh or unsafe pet handling claims
- good fit for beginner pet owners

## Best first offer

Use one of these:

- clearly labeled partner listing
- sponsored guide mention
- local resource placement
- promo-code commission
- manual referral agreement

## Simple outreach note

Hi,

I’m building PetNeeds.ai, a family-friendly pet care website with simple guides for dogs, cats, fish, birds, reptiles, small pets, senior pets, and rescue pets.

We are preparing early partner listings and promo-code commission options for pet-safe businesses.

Would you be open to discussing a clearly labeled listing or sponsored guide mention?

Thank you,
Gerry
PetNeeds.ai

## Manual tracking fields

Use a private spreadsheet with:

- business name
- category
- website
- contact method
- date contacted
- response
- next follow-up
- approved or rejected
- notes

Do not store private outreach lists inside the public repo once real contacts are added.
