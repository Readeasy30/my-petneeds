# PetNeeds.ai Advertiser and Commission Program

## Purpose

Create a safe way for pet brands, local pet businesses, and service providers to promote useful products or services on PetNeeds.ai.

This is a planning and sales document. Do not add live ads, live tracking scripts, payments, or affiliate links until reviewed and approved.

## Best first model

Start with direct partnerships instead of complex ad networks.

Direct partnerships are easier to understand, easier to approve, and easier to keep family-safe.

## Partner types

### 1. Local pet businesses

Examples:

- groomers
- trainers
- pet stores
- boarding services
- dog walkers
- pet photographers
- rescue groups
- local pet events

Best offer:

Featured local resource listing or sponsored local guide placement.

### 2. Pet product companies

Examples:

- pet beds
- bowls
- leashes
- crates
- carriers
- toys
- grooming tools
- aquarium supplies
- bird cage accessories
- reptile habitat supplies

Best offer:

Sponsored product mention, product guide placement, or direct commission promo code.

### 3. Educational or service partners

Examples:

- dog training courses
- pet insurance information partners
- pet first aid courses
- pet sitting directories
- adoption/shelter support organizations

Best offer:

Sponsored resource card, educational guide sponsor, or lead referral commission.

## Direct commission options

### Option A — Promo code commission

Partner gives PetNeeds.ai a unique promo code.

Example format:

`PETNEEDS10`

Partner pays a commission for orders using that code.

Best for small businesses because it does not require tracking scripts.

### Option B — Manual lead commission

PetNeeds.ai sends a visitor to a local business through a contact form, email intro, or tracked inquiry.

Partner pays a set amount per qualified lead or new customer.

Best for groomers, trainers, boarding, local stores, and services.

### Option C — Sponsored listing fee

Partner pays a monthly fee for a clearly labeled listing.

Best for local businesses that want simple visibility.

### Option D — Sponsored guide placement

Partner sponsors a helpful guide page.

Example:

This dog daily routine guide is sponsored by [local pet store].

Must be clearly disclosed.

## Starter pricing ideas

Use these only as starting points. Adjust after traffic grows.

### Early launch pricing

- Local resource listing: $25 to $75 per month
- Featured local listing: $75 to $150 per month
- Sponsored guide mention: $100 to $250 per month
- Product guide placement: $50 to $200 per month
- Direct commission: 5% to 20% of tracked sale
- Local qualified lead: $10 to $50 per qualified inquiry

## Commission tracking without scripts

Start simple:

1. Unique promo code
2. Partner monthly sales report
3. Manual inquiry tracker
4. Dedicated landing page if needed
5. Monthly invoice or payment request

Avoid tracking scripts until the site has a clear privacy and disclosure setup.

## Disclosure rules

Any paid relationship must be clear to visitors.

Use labels like:

- Sponsored
- Partner Listing
- Paid Placement
- PetNeeds.ai may earn a commission

Place the disclosure near the promotion, not hidden at the bottom.

## Product acceptance rules

Only promote products that fit PetNeeds.ai's family-safe and pet-safe direction.

Reject:

- unsafe pet products
- shock collars or harsh training tools
- products with unsupported medical claims
- fake reviews
- counterfeit products
- products aimed at treating illness without veterinary oversight
- products with poor customer service reputation
- anything that conflicts with pet safety language

## Review checklist before accepting partner

- Is the product or service pet-appropriate?
- Is the claim safe and reasonable?
- Can the partner provide clear terms?
- Is there a simple way to track commission or leads?
- Is the disclosure clear?
- Does the promotion help visitors instead of distracting them?
- Would this damage trust with pet owners?

## Best launch offer

Start with this simple offer:

PetNeeds.ai Partner Listing

Includes:

- clear sponsored label
- short business or product description
- category placement
- link to partner website
- optional promo code
- no guarantee of sales
- monthly review
