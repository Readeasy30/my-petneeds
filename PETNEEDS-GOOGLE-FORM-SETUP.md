# PetNeeds.ai Google Form Setup

## Purpose

Create a controlled pet photo submission process where every photo is reviewed before public display.

## Form name

PetNeeds.ai Reviewed Pet Photo Submission

## Form description

Share a pet photo for possible future PetNeeds.ai community features. Photos are reviewed before anything is published. Submission does not guarantee publication.

## Recommended settings

- Collect email addresses.
- Limit to one response if appropriate.
- Allow one image upload.
- Keep uploads private until reviewed.
- Connect responses to a Google Sheet.
- Do not publish the file-upload folder publicly.

## Required questions

1. First name
2. Last initial only
3. Email address
4. Pet name
5. Pet type
6. Pet age if known
7. Pet category
8. Upload one pet photo
9. One kind, family-friendly sentence about your pet
10. Permission and safety confirmations

## Pet category choices

- Dog
- Cat
- Fish
- Bird
- Reptile
- Small pet
- Senior pet
- Rescue pet
- Other

## Required permission checkboxes

- I own this photo or have permission to share it.
- I give PetNeeds.ai permission to review this photo for possible public display.
- I understand submission does not guarantee publication.
- I understand PetNeeds.ai may edit the caption for clarity, kindness, or safety.
- I confirm the photo is family-friendly.
- I confirm the photo does not show private information.
- I confirm the photo does not show unsafe animal handling.
- I confirm no people are clearly shown unless I have permission for them to appear.

## Review sheet columns

- Date received
- First name
- Last initial
- Email
- Pet name
- Pet type
- Caption
- File link
- Permission confirmed
- Review status
- Reviewer notes
- Approved caption
- Publish category
- Publish date
- Removal request

## Review status values

- New
- Needs review
- Approved
- Needs edit
- Rejected
- Published
- Removed

## Public display rule

Do not publish full names, email addresses, addresses, phone numbers, or private details.

Use approved public display names only.

Example:

Buddy the rescue dog

## Launch recommendation

For Wednesday launch, keep the public website page as review-rules only. Add the actual form link after one test submission is reviewed successfully.
