# Pet Photo Safety Review Workflow

## Purpose

PetNeeds.ai may invite pet photo submissions later, but photos must be reviewed before anything is shown publicly.

This file defines the safety review process.

## Current status

Photo uploads are not automatic public posts.

`upload-photo.html` is a planning and community-rules page until a controlled review system is ready.

## Recommended safe setup

Use a Google Form or similar form tool first.

Required form fields:

- first name
- last initial only
- email address
- pet name
- pet type
- photo upload
- short story or caption
- ownership confirmation
- permission to review and possibly publish
- agreement that no people are shown unless specifically approved
- agreement that the photo is family-friendly

## Review sheet columns

Use a spreadsheet with these columns:

- date received
- submitter name
- email
- pet name
- pet type
- file link
- caption/story
- review status
- reviewer notes
- publish category
- approved public name
- approved caption
- publish date
- removal request received

## Review status values

Use only these status values:

- New
- Needs review
- Approved
- Needs edit
- Rejected
- Published
- Removed

## Reject photos that include

- nudity or sexual content
- hate symbols or hateful messages
- violence, injury, blood, or cruelty
- unsafe handling of animals
- visible private information
- children or people without clear permission
- offensive gestures or language
- poor image quality that makes review impossible
- stolen, watermarked, or suspicious images
- wild animals being handled in unsafe ways
- medical distress shown without educational context and review

## Extra caution categories

These need extra review before publication:

- reptiles found outside
- wildlife photos
- injured pets
- sick pets
- children with pets
- service animals
- photos showing home addresses, license plates, school names, phone numbers, or documents

## Approval checklist

Before approving, confirm:

- The image appears family-friendly.
- The pet is the main subject.
- No private information is visible.
- No unsafe animal handling is shown.
- No people are clearly visible unless permission is documented.
- The caption is kind and non-offensive.
- The submitter granted permission.
- The pet category is clear.

## Publication rule

Never publish automatically.

Only publish manually after review.

Use the approved public name and approved caption, not the raw submission text.

## Removal rule

If a submitter asks for removal, remove the photo from public display and mark status as Removed.

## AI assistance rule

AI may help summarize captions or flag possible review issues, but a human review is required before public display.

Do not rely on AI-only approval for public photos.
