Puppy Starter Checklist

A calm first-week checklist for families bringing home a puppy. Start with food, water, rest, potty rhythm, sleep space, and simple jobs.

Open Puppy Supplies Dog Routine Guide

First-week rule

Keep the routine simple. Puppies learn faster when meals, potty trips, play, and rest happen in a steady rhythm.

Do less, but do it daily

A puppy does not need a complicated first week. A calm home, safe supplies, short routines, and family notes are enough to begin.

Setup

  • Food and water bowls
  • Quiet sleep spot
  • Leash and collar or harness
  • ID tag
  • Safe chew toys
  • Cleaning supplies

Daily Routine

  • Meals
  • Fresh water
  • Nap time
  • Play time
  • Potty routine
  • Calm bedtime

Family Jobs

Give each person one simple job. Puppies learn faster when the home routine is steady.

  • One person handles meals
  • One person checks water
  • One person tracks potty trips
  • Adults supervise children
