Puppy Starter Checklist

A calm first-week checklist for families bringing home a puppy.

Puppy safety reminder

For trouble breathing, injury, poisoning risk, repeated vomiting, diarrhea with weakness, not eating, collapse, or sudden behavior changes, contact a veterinarian or emergency clinic.

Open the safety guide →

Setup

  • Food and water bowls
  • Quiet sleep spot
  • Leash and collar
  • ID tag
  • Simple toys
  • Cleaning supplies

Daily Routine

  • Meals
  • Fresh water
  • Nap time
  • Play time
  • Potty routine
  • Calm bedtime

Family Jobs

Give each person one simple job. Puppies learn faster when the home routine is steady.

  • One person handles meals
  • One person checks water
  • One person tracks potty trips
  • Adults supervise children
