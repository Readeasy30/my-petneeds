# PetNeeds.ai Growth Plan

## Goal

Build PetNeeds.ai into a simple, trusted pet-help website with clear pages, useful guides, community ideas, and future revenue options.

## Current foundation

- Static HTML site
- Plain CSS
- Simple navigation
- Pet category pages
- Support pages
- Sitemap
- Site checklist

## Audience

- Families with pets
- New pet owners
- Older pet owners
- People adopting rescue pets
- Beginners with fish, birds, reptiles, and small pets

## Content plan

1. Keep each guide simple.
2. Use short paragraphs.
3. Avoid confusing medical language.
4. Link serious concerns to the safety page.
5. Add one useful page at a time.
6. Keep pages easy to read on a phone.

## Future revenue ideas

- Helpful product roundups
- Clearly disclosed affiliate links
- Local resource pages
- Sponsored educational guides
- Email list growth
- Printable guides

## Do not add yet

- Live ads
- Tracking scripts
- Payment handling
- Private keys
- Complex frameworks
- Unsupported care claims

## Next useful pages

- New pet owner checklist
- Puppy starter checklist
- Kitten starter checklist
- Fish tank starter checklist
- Senior pet comfort checklist
- Rescue pet first week checklist
