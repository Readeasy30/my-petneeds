Pet Family Notes Guide

A simple family checklist for writing down pet details, routines, and questions.

Pet Details

  • Pet name
  • Pet type
  • Age or life stage
  • Weight if known
  • Regular food

Daily Routine Notes

  • Meal times
  • Water habits
  • Bathroom routine
  • Sleep pattern
  • Activity level

Change Notes

  • What changed?
  • When did it start?
  • Was there a new food, visitor, trip, or routine?
  • Is the pet acting like usual?
  • What questions should the family ask?

Local Contact Notes

  • Regular pet-care contact
  • Nearby after-hours contact
  • Microchip number if available
  • Important family notes
  • Helpful links or phone numbers
This guide is for organizing family notes and planning questions.
