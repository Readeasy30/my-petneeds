Pet Family Notes Guide

A simple notes guide for writing down pet details, routines, changes, questions, contacts, and family jobs in one place.

Open Starter Guides Build a Pet Question

Why notes help

When the whole family writes things down, it is easier to spot changes, share jobs, and ask clearer questions.

Pet Details

  • Pet name
  • Pet type
  • Age or life stage
  • Size or weight if known
  • Regular food
  • Important likes and dislikes

Daily Routine Notes

  • Meal times
  • Water habits
  • Bathroom routine
  • Sleep pattern
  • Activity level
  • Family care jobs

Change Notes

  • What changed?
  • When did it start?
  • Was there a new food, visitor, trip, or routine?
  • Is the pet acting like usual?
  • What questions should the family ask?

Local Contact Notes

  • Regular pet-care contact
  • Nearby after-hours contact
  • Microchip number if available
  • Important family notes
  • Helpful links or phone numbers
